Who We Are

NACT was founded in 2016 and is in the productization of gamechanging technologies through the form of robotics accessories.

We are proficient in supplying drone accessories such as tethered drone systems, light cores, lightning drones, and other devices to strategically enhance drone capability be it for users of off-the-shelf drones or supplying to drone OEMs. We do also provide robotics services to customers if the requirement is a niche, after which we train and empower our customers to provide trial-proven services.

Our intimate knowledge of drone and robotics systems puts us at the forefront of a burgeoning mechanical revolution. And our track record speaks for itself.
